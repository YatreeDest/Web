Refund Policy

Your 100% satisfaction is important for us. At Yatree Destination, we do our best to provide the most accurate rides descriptions we can. Each ride pages has a size guide that you can refer to along with services specifications.

We do have refund policies for our valuable customers under the following situations:

  • If you CANCEL any of your rides before 15 minutes of start, we will refund the full amount. Note that, Once your ride gets started we have all the rights to refuse refunds.
  • Once the ride gets started there will be NO REFUND initiated.
  • For any other ride related or refund related issues, kindly contact our support team via support@yatreedestination.com

Cancellation Policy

  • Customer cannot cancel the ride/trip while commuting, if needed, kindly contact our support team via support@yatreedestination.com

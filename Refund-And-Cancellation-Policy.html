Refund Policy

Cancellation requests must be made in writing to the Yatree Destination Private Limited company via email or call. All mails must be sent to support@yatreedestination.com and cancellation acknowledgement will be sent within 24 hours and amount refunded within 7 to 10 working days subject to below refund policy.

Rental Services

  • 70% of the total amount will be refunded if cancelled a day prior to the event.
  • 50% of the total amount will be refunded if cancelled on the scheduled day.
  • For any other ride related or refund related issues, kindly contact our support team via support@yatreedestination.com

Spin Around Rides

  • 70% of the total amount will be refunded if cancelled before 2 hours of the trip.
  • 50% of the total amount will be refunded if cancelled within 2 hours of the scheduled ride.
  • For any other ride related or refund related issues, kindly contact our support team via support@yatreedestination.com

Payment Option

  • You may deposit the money directly to Yatree Destination A/c, or through Payment Gateway if using Website and App for the booking.
  • The amount will be refunded through original payment mode only.
  • COD option is also available and you may contact us at info@yatreedestination.com for more clarification on COD and other payment options.
  • For any other ride related or refund related issues, kindly contact our support team via support@yatreedestination.com

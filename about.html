Introducting the Concept

We are here to celebrate the beauty of the historic capital of Mewar by delivering a magical voyage accompanied with luxury via a sustainable mode of transport i.e. E-rickshaw.

The whole concept Yatree Destination revolves around making the city a pollution free tourist hub. The essence of the city and its never ending beauty have inpired us in taking an attempt of bringing out to you a feasible solution , which comes up in form of E - rickshaws. Our E- rickshaws aims to give tourists a venturesome ride that they would never forget. Accompanied with luxury , these rickshaws also possess the tales of valour and chivalry printed on them.

‘The city of lakes ‘ also called the ‘Venice of the east’ has its own history and we are thrilled to show it to you with our vision.
We hope to make your day better!

Why E-Rickshaw

As the country continues to progress, E-rickshaws have turned to be a logical and environmental friendly solution. As you explore this romantic city, E - rickshaws can make your excursion calm, worthy and pollution free.

With increasing levels of pollution there is a need to come with such solution which could help us control the pollution and would also be able to meet the needs of tourists.

Our E-rickshaws are personally customised to meet every need of the tourist and also give them a cleaner way to show them around.

Sustainability

Yatree Destination commits itself to a totally electrical, zero-emission platform, with 100% of journeys in zero, public or micro emissions vehicles. As the new mobility platform in India, it is our obligation to fight climate change more aggressively. We will do so by providing riders more opportunities to go green, enabling drivers to go electric, prioritising clarity and supporting the private sector to accelerate an energy transition that is fair and clear.

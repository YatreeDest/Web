About Yatree Destination

Yatree destination is here to celebrate the beauty of the historic capital of Mewar by delivering a magical voyage accompanied with luxury via a sustainable mode of transport i.e. E-rickshaw.

The whole concept of Yatree Destination revolves around ‘Going Green’ and giving an incredible experience by ensuring a superior user experience and customer service with a unique concept of tourism and daily hassle free commute. Yatree Destination aims to build a one stop solution for travellers and help them explore all the tourist attractions of the city and give them a venturesome ride through one of the most beautiful city.


#Be Unstoppable

Why E-Rickshaw

As the modernisation continues to progress, E-rickshaws have turned to be a logical and environmental friendly solution. As you explore this romantic city, E - rickshaws can make your excursion calm, worthy and pollution free. Our E-rickshaws are personally customised to meet every need of the tourist and also give them a cleaner way to show them around.

E rickshaw becomes one of the most integral and crucial part of public transport and runs on Battery which is rechargeable by electricity, which keep its running cost lower and also reduces very less emission.

Sustainability

Yatree Destination commits itself to a totally electrical, zero-emission platform, with 100% of journeys in zero, public or micro emission vehicles. As the new mobility platform in India, it is our obligation to fight climate change more aggressively.

We will do so by providing riders more opportunities to go green, enabling drivers to go electric, prioritising clarity and supporting the private sector to accelerate an energy transition that is fair and clear.

Yatree Destination Fleet

Get your Ride at your doorstep

Yatree Destination fleet consists of 12 E-rickshaws that are meant to give you a familiar auto ride without any hassle and haggling for price. Our E-rickshaws are a step towards modernisation and growth and well equipped with all the amenities to meet the needs of every traveller. Our E-rickshaws are exclusively designed with Graffiti to give tourist a glimpse of the art and culture of the city.

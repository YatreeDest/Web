Spin Around Rides

Spin around with Yatree Destination and give a one stop solution to all your travel needs. Take a ‘Spin Around’ one of the most eye pleasing city and nurture the essence of travel with us. Take a step further and rent an E- rickshaw to schedule a travel as per your selection criterion and requirements. We, at Yatree destination are willing to help you to make your day as you want it to be.

Fateh Sagar

(60 Minutes Ride)

Spin around Fatehsagar lake and take a walkthrough around the serene waters of Fatehsagar along with Nehru Garden, Moti Magri, Rani road and taste the delicacy of the street food of the city. A drive around the lake is a pleasing experience and is a best introduction to the natural beauty of Udaipur.

Badi Lake

(120 Minutes Ride)

Take a spin around ‘Badi lake’ and drown in the beauty of lush green aravalis and a picturesque view of the lake.
Situated at a distance from 12 kms from the city the badi lake gives a never ending serene and calm atmosphere with natural beauty all around.
